Recipe Organizer

A simple yet efficient web application designed for temporarily organizing and storing recipes📝🍳. This platform allows users to easily input essential recipe details such as the recipe name, ingredients, and cooking instructions through an intuitive and interactive form📋💡.

With the handy "Add Item" feature, users can save multiple recipes within their session, making it the perfect tool for jotting down new recipes or meal ideas on the go⏳🍲. Additionally, each recipe has a "Delete" button that allows users to remove individual recipes as needed, giving full control over their collection🗑️✨.

The application offers a hassle-free experience, as the data persists only for the current session, ensuring that your recipe collection stays organized without any long-term commitments. Once the session ends, the details reset, providing a fresh slate for your next recipe adventure.

Whether you're experimenting with new dishes or trying to keep track of family favorites, the Recipe Organizer is here to make your cooking journey a little more organized and a lot more fun! 🌟👩‍🍳

Tech Stack: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
